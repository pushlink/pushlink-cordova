idle(successCallback, errorCallback)

Especially useful for NINJA strategy. In order to update your app only when it is idle: 1 - Call PushLink.idle(false) before PushLink.start() 2 - Call PushLink.idle(true) when your app become idle 3 - Call PushLink.idle(false) when your app is back to the action again When idle == false it disables all strategies
Parameters:
